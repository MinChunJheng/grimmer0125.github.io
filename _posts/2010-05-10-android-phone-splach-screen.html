--- title: 更改Android手機開機畫面 date: '2010-05-10T22:59:00.003+08:00' author: Teng-Chieh Kang tags: - Android modified_time: '2010-05-21T18:39:28.668+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2031747454610917099.post-5347266615531481419 blogger_orig_url: http://q-and-g.blogspot.com/2010/05/android.html --- 我相信很多人都看首頁的Hami圖案不爽很久了, 以下是更改步驟
0. 你的機子需有 Engineering SPL or HardSPL
1. 準備320x480的一張圖
2. 下載paint.net的plugin
http://forums.getpaint.net/index.php?/topic/16747-rgb565-android-splash-screen-format/?s=c90b6849b93125d20d28869197f1dcb9
然後放到paint.net的filetype資料夾下
3. 使用paint.net把圖轉成Android所吃的rgb565格式
4.參考以下這篇用fastboot刷開機畫面的splash區
http://reviews.risesoftware.com/pg/blog/I_Artist/read/261/how-to-change-your-boot-screen-image-on-htc-dream-and-htc-magic
5.done.
